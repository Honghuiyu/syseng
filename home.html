Project Overview

We are one of three teams working on the Spot Markets project. The overall project aims to revolutionise the high street by providing shoppers with location and time-relevant notifications. An example of this would be looking into the shopper's purchase history and seeing that they bought a pair of red shoes last week. Due to this data, the application would suggest to the user that they purchase a corresponding red handbag when they pass a shop selling one with a 10% discount.

Our extension to this project is a tourism layer that uses the data harvested for the shopping notifications to recommend relevant tourist attractions that a user may find interesting. The object is to make these notifications as relevant as possible at a time that makes most sense.

Meet the squad

We are three second year undergraduate Computer Science students. Our project is in collaboration with Microsoft and Kent County Council.
